# Question Bank Authoring Standard

Status: active

## Non-negotiable rule

Production questions are hand-authored static content. No script, template,
formula loop, placeholder suffix, choice shuffler, or runtime prompt builder may
create production questions. Scripts may only audit or validate authored data.

## Official basis

The bank follows the 2026 CSC Professional-level scope:

- General Information: Philippine Constitution, R.A. 6713, peace and human
  rights, and environment management and protection
- Verbal Ability in English and Filipino: word meaning, sentence completion,
  error recognition, sentence structure, paragraph organization, and reading
  comprehension
- Numerical Ability in English: basic operations, number sequence, and word
  problems
- Analytical Ability in English: word analogy, symbolic/abstract reasoning,
  assumptions and conclusions, and data interpretation

Primary scope source:

`https://csc.gov.ph/phocadownload/userupload/erpo/announcements/2026/ExamAnn_2026_03_Conduct%20of%2009%20Aug%202026%20CSE-PPT_orig%20signed.pdf`

CSC states that the Professional paper contains 150 Test Proper items and 20
EDQ items. This reviewer intentionally uses 170 scored practice items because
that is the approved product format; it must not claim that this section split
or scoring arrangement is official.

## Per-version blueprint

Each of the 20 versions contains exactly 170 questions:

| Items | Section | Count | Required mix |
| --- | --- | ---: | --- |
| 1-20 | General Information | 20 | Constitution 6, R.A. 6713 6, peace/human rights 4, environment 4 |
| 21-80 | Verbal Ability | 60 | all six official skills; at least 18 Filipino-language items |
| 81-120 | Numerical Ability | 40 | operations 10, sequences 8, varied word problems 22 |
| 121-170 | Analytical Ability | 50 | analogy 10, abstract/symbolic 10, assumptions/conclusions 14, data interpretation 16 |

The 16 data-interpretation items use four independent shared stimuli with four
questions each. At least two are plain tables. Charts must include a title,
units, readable labels, values, legend when multiple series exist, and an exact
text/table alternative. A decorative chart with unexplained colors is invalid.

## Difficulty rubric

Target per version: 35-50 easy, 80-100 medium, and 30-45 hard.

- Easy: one fact, one language rule, or one arithmetic step
- Medium: contextual application, two operations, or one clear inference
- Hard: multi-step reasoning, close but defensible distractors, paragraph
  organization, compound conditions, or multi-row data interpretation

Difficulty is assigned from the reasoning required, not from large numbers,
obscure vocabulary, or deliberately confusing wording.

## Item quality rules

Every question must have:

- a stable version-specific ID and item number
- exactly four distinct, plausible choices
- one unambiguously correct answer
- a teaching explanation that proves the answer and rejects the key trap
- section, official skill, subtopic, difficulty, and expected-time metadata
- provenance identifying an official source, an authored quantitative check,
  or an original language/reasoning construction
- `needs_review` status until a separate human content review is complete

Reject an item when it:

- repeats a stem or differs only by names, numbers, or shuffled choices
- includes `Practice set`, `item #`, a version number, or other fake uniqueness
- depends on unstated assumptions, regional slang, trivia, or changing facts
- uses duplicate, overlapping, joke, generic filler, or obviously weak choices
- asks `how many more` in the wrong direction
- has more than one valid logic arrangement or conclusion
- requires a calculator, specialist knowledge, or memorizing a non-scope fact
- copies a commercial reviewer, leaked item, or purported official question

## Source policy

Legal and general-information items use primary official sources such as the
1987 Constitution, Lawphil/Official Gazette statutes, CHR materials, and DENR
or EMB materials. Public reviewer sites may inform format and pacing only;
their wording, scenarios, choices, and explanations must not be copied.

All questions are original independent practice content and must retain the
non-affiliation disclaimer.
